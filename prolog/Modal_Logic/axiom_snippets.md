## Modal Operators & Modes
- **Three Validity Modes** (Appendix_Phenomenology.tex:187-230): Subjective ($\mathsf{S}$) = Carspecken "I-feeling"; Objective ($\mathsf{O}$) = structure of object for consciousness; Normative ($\mathsf{N}$) = Brandom commitments/entitlements mediating S and O. Unity only at "silence" state $U$.
- **Subjective Operators** (Appendix_Phenomenology.tex:232-310):
  - $\SBoxDown$: necessary contraction/First Negation; examples include fixation on "Pure Being".
  - $\SDiaUp$: possibility of release; "choice point" intuition of letting go.
  - $\SBoxUp$: necessary expansion after letting go; "Aha" integration.
  - $\SDiaDown$: possibility of sliding back into fixation.
- **Objective Operators** (Appendix_Phenomenology.tex:312-360+): compression = crystallization ($\OBoxDown$), expansion = liquefaction ($\OBoxUp$), plus corresponding possibility operators $\ODiaUp$, $\ODiaDown$.
- **Normative Operators** (Appendix_Phenomenology.tex:360+): $\NBoxDown$ solidifies laws; $\NDiaUp$ opens norms to critique; $\NBoxUp$ enacts necessary revolutionary expansion; $\NDiaDown$ signals drift back toward rigid codes.
